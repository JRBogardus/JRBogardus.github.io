Toxicity in gaming is when people, usually young males, make fun of someone repeatedly in an online game, which includes sexual harassment, racism, threats of violence, privacy violations, etc. It is important to understand so that we can stop it from spreading. Ways to stop toxicity in gaming can be as simple as saying, “Don’t be toxic,” or you can report them as well.
